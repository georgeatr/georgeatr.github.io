Game Projects

Leaf Lad Polluted Lands

Global Game Jam 2019. Take the role of Leaf Lad and embark on a quest to save the world from pollution. Advance through 4 distinct levels each with their own enemies and take on GlobalWarMan to complete your quest.

Remember Me

This game is based on the story "Hanasaki Jiji". You play as the spirit of a dog called Will, his main goal is to collect the memories of the past to be able to remember what happened. Bring Will back to life and find treasure while you venture through different worlds and environments. (This game is WIP)

Project Nano

Project Nano was created during the 2020 Global Game Jam. The main theme was HOME. This game is a top down 2D rouge like shooter in which you take control of Nano a nanobot and your main goal is to heal the body from all the viruses you encounter. (This game is a prototype)

Run Agarry Run

This small game is a personal project which I used to learn different aspects of Unity2D. As an inifinite runner game, run and jump as Agarry through space while you collect stars and earn the highest score possible.

Cookie Rage

Cookie Rage was my first Unity3D project. I worked together with 2 of my friends to make a 3D shooter. Play as a Gangster Donut with a single goal: Kill all the zombie cookies.
